In June 2021, I spent a month traveling Europe. I met some of the most incredible people (some of who are shown in this gallery). These photos are some of my first experiements with film photography. The film captures the experience in a much different capacity than digital photography. These photos reflect some of the greatest moments of my life; they give me so much joy and I hope they can do the same for you.
